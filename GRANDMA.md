# 👵 Hey Grandma! Let me explain what I built.

## What is this?

You know how when you want to go on a holiday, you have to spend hours searching the internet to figure out where to go, what to pack, and what to do each day? 

**I built a little website that does all of that for you — in seconds!**

---

## How does it work?

1. You open the website on your phone or computer.
2. You type in **how much money you want to spend**, **how many days you'll be traveling**, and **what kind of place you like** — the beach, a big city, or the mountains.
3. You press a button.
4. A very smart computer brain (called AI — Artificial Intelligence) reads your answers and comes up with a full holiday plan just for you!

---

## What does it give you?

- 🏖️ **A destination idea** — where to go and why it's perfect for you
- 📅 **A day-by-day plan** — what to do every morning, afternoon, and evening
- 🎒 **A packing list** — so you don't forget your sunscreen!
- 🖼️ **A beautiful scene description** — like a postcard picture in words

---

## Is it safe?

Yes! The website never stores your personal information. It just reads what you type, asks the AI, and shows you the answer. That's it.
